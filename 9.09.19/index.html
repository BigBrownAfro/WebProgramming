CSS Measurement Units

  • %: Defines a Measurement
  • cm: centimeters
  • in: inches
  • mm: millimeters
  • px: pixels
  • pt: points (1/72 of an inch)
  • em: 12 points
  • ex: relative to a font's 'x' height
  • pc: in picas - a pica is 12 points
  • vw: Viewport width, percentage of browser's width
  • vh: Viewport height, percentage of browser's width
